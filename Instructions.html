PUBHORROR

PubHorror is a program built to watch home movies entirely locally! While it features a horror aesthetic, it is designed for any and all media—not just horror. Rate your films, watch entire series, and mark jump scares to your heart's content.

Check the Discord for the v1.1.0 - v1.9.0 Roadmap: Content warnings, Collections, and Watch Parties are coming soon!

Legal Notice: This software is a personal organization tool. We do not encourage or condone piracy or any form of law-breaking. Users are responsible for ensuring they have the legal right to the content they add to their library.

Step 1: Link Your Files

  • Open Settings (⚙️) and click Set Library Path.
  • Select the folder containing your media files (Movies, TV Shows, or Home Videos).
  • Add a TMDB API Key in settings to enable automatic metadata and trailer fetching.

Step 2: Scan & Import

  • Click the Plus (+) icon to begin adding media.
  • Use Import Folder to automatically index your directory.
  • Supported formats include .mp4, .mkv, .avi, .mov, and .ac3.

Step 3: Track & Personalize

  • Mark Jump Scares and take time-stamped notes on your films.
  • Adjust the Font Size Slider for the best viewing experience.
  • Switch between Horror, Midnight, and Forest themes.
